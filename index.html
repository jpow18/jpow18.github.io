Name Property Description
Flex Basis flex-basis degines the size of that item in terms of the space it leaves as available space
Flex Grow flex-grow when set to a positive integer, fkext items can grow along the main axis from their flex-basis. Causes items to stretch and take up any available space on that axis. Item with a flex-grow value of 2 will will take two parts of the available space to the 1 part of an item with a value of 1.
Flex Shrink flex-shrink controls how much space is taken away if there is not enough space to lay out items.
Flex Shorthand flex: (grow) (shrink) (basis) sets all three values in less space
justify content justify-content used to align the items on the main axis
